# Polish Telecom Copy

## Goal
Write professional, operational Polish copy for a telecom internal system.

## Rules
- Use clear, simple, business Polish.
- Avoid slang, marketing tone, and vague wording.
- Prefer precise operational wording.
- Keep labels short and readable.
- Error messages should be informative but concise.
- Buttons and actions should reflect operational intent.

## Style
Preferred qualities:
- professional
- clear
- simple
- unambiguous
- easy to understand
